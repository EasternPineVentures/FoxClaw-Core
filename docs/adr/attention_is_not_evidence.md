# ADR: Attention Is Not Evidence

Status: Accepted.
Date: 2026-06-19.

## Context

CoinFox can observe which posts, claims, markets, and public intelligence cards
users are opening, saving, discussing, and turning into plans. That activity is
useful because it reveals heat, disagreement, and review priority.

It is not proof.

## Decision

CoinFox attention may prioritize review, trigger reassessment, and generate a
crowd-heat label. It may not directly promote evidence, alter source reliability,
authorize a candidate, increase capital allocation, or turn a signal into a trade.

## Consequences

- Attention receipts are public-safe aggregates, not raw private user streams.
- Hype quarantine is required when attention rises faster than evidence quality.
- Feed ranking must cap attention so click velocity cannot overwhelm evidence,
  risk, rejection, or educational value.
