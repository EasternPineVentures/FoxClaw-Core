# Press Experiment

Can a receipt-driven decision system find mispriced probabilities, survive real costs, and
cover its own operating expenses?

Good public framing:

```text
Can FoxClaw Pay Its Own Rent?
Every Forecast. Every Fee. Every Failure.
FoxClaw vs the Crowd.
We Gave the Fox a Ledger, Not a Wallet.
```

Do not use:

```text
guaranteed profit
passive-income machine
easy money
sure bet
can't lose
legal everywhere
AI prints money
```

If the experiment fails, publish that clearly. The honesty is part of the product.
