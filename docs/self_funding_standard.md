# Self-Funding Standard

Status: Phase F foundation.

The phrase `FoxClaw pays for itself` is gated by receipts, not vibes.

The current standard requires:

```text
minimum_consecutive_days = 90
minimum_resolved_positions = 250
minimum_self_funding_ratio = 1.25
positive net economic profit
complete cost receipts
founder_live mode
```

Paper results may qualify only as an experimental self-funding candidate. They may not be
called verified self-funding or durability verified.

Missing costs, zero costs, tiny samples, insufficient days, or paper mode deny the verified
claim even if paper P&L is positive.
